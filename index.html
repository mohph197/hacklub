ABOUT THE EVENT
Hacklub, a two days event organized by CSE, will take place at ESI. It will have a conference about: How to create a club and the importance of it. In addition to different workshops and challenges from different departments that a club can have. The participants will be devised into different teams, the team who collects more points from participating in the challenges will be the winning one. Which will get a special gift from our club at the end.
FAQ
Why should you join the event?
If you’re interested in creating a new club we will guide you through by showing you all the steps you need to pass by and if you’re already a member in one we will help you to improve your club’s workflow by the different workshops and challenges that we will provide.
Who can join?
Everyone can join the event, mostly students who would like to learn more about how to create a new club or improve the workflow of their club.
How to join?
All you need to do is to fill the registration form.
Where will it take place?
It will take place at ESI ecole nationale supérieure d’informatique.
What if I don’t have a team for the challenge?
Not to worry, we will help you find teammates after announcing the challenges.
